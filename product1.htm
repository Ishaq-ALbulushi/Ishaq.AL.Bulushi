Residential Trash Cans

When seeking durable and reliable trash cans for household waste management, especially larger ones, consider options like the Simplehuman Rectangular Step Can, Rubbermaid Brute Rollout Trash Can, Toter Residential Heavy Duty Trash Can, iTouchless SoftStep Stainless Steel Step Trash Can, and Rubbermaid Step-On Wastebasket. The Simplehuman Rectangular Step Can, available in 45-50 liters, features a stainless steel build, step pedal for hands-free use, soft-close lid, and fingerprint-proof finish, ensuring high durability with a strong pedal mechanism. The Rubbermaid Brute Rollout Trash Can, ranging from 50-100 liters, is made of high-density polyethylene, includes wheels for easy mobility, a hinged lid, and sturdy handles, providing exceptional durability for both indoor and outdoor use. The Toter Residential Heavy Duty Trash Can boasts a massive 96-gallon capacity, rugged rim technology, advanced rotational molding, large wheels for maneuverability, and an ergonomic handle, making it extremely durable for long-term use even under harsh conditions. The iTouchless SoftStep Stainless Steel Step Trash Can, with a 49-liter capacity, offers a stainless steel body, step pedal, removable inner bucket, soft-close lid, and odor control system, combining sleek design with robust functionality.
